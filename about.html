About Me

Francesco Di Credico

I’m Strategy Director at LiveXtension and a lecturer at Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore, where I contribute to exploring the connections between digital innovation, sustainability, and the role of technology in enhancing well-being. Over the past decade, I’ve guided branding and communication strategies that merge creativity and data to achieve lasting, positive impact.

Fascinated by the interplay between the human mind and technology, I delve into how artificial intelligence can refine marketing and enrich our lives. In my free time, I write and share thoughts on the future of innovation and sustainability, always driven by the belief in the transformative power of ideas and authentic collaboration.

Let’s connect and explore new perspectives together.
